Cartoon Network is an American cable television channel owned by the Cartoon Network, Inc., a division of the Kids, Young Adults and Classics division of Warner Bros. Entertainment, itself a subsidiary of AT&T's WarnerMedia through its Studios and Networks Group division.

Show Name- Ben ten, Ten-year-old Ben Tennyson discovers a mysterious device named the Omnitrix on a family vacation. The device allows him to transform into 10 different alien forms replete with unique superpowers.
Show Name- Scooby doo, Brainiac Velma, jock Fred, fashionista Daphne, hippie Shaggy, and Shaggy's highstrung, talking Great Dane, Scooby-Doo, climb into their green van, the Mystery Machine, and hit the road in search of weird phenomena to solve. And even when Scooby and the gang aren't actively searching for them, mysteries just have a way of falling into their laps. Ruh-roh!.
Show Name- Johnny Bravo, Johnny Bravo has no shortage of confidence, and he's certain that all women want him. With his big hair and tight black clothes, he's known for his karate chops, accompanied by the sound of a bullwhip.
Show Name- Oggy and the cockroaches, Lazy cat Oggy loves to watch TV and eat food. However, his flatmates, who happen to be three tiny cockroaches, enjoy attacking Oggy's refrigerator and creating a messy situation.
Show Name- Tom and Jerry, Tom, a house cat, embarks on his adventure of persistently pursuing his never-ending quest of nabbing a notorious mouse, Jerry.
Show Name- Johnny Test, Young Johnny is gung-ho and full of courage. Johnny's brainiac twin sisters, Susan and Mary, use Johnny as their guinea pig for their outrageous scientific experiments. If they can dream it up, Johnny will do it; as long as his genetically engineered super dog, Dukey, can come along.
